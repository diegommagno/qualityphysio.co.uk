About Us

Quality Physio was founded by Paulo Cesar Martinelli Filho, a post graduated physiotherapist, with 13 years experience, HCPC Registered and Member of the CSP (Chartered Society Physiotherapy).

We provide home visits physiotherapy wherever you need and when you need it.
We are able to provide all the usual clinic equipment to you at your home, at the nursing home, at the residential home.

Back Pain
Neck, shoulders, elbows, knees and ankles injuries.
Neuro Rehabilitation

Sports Injuries, Sports massages
